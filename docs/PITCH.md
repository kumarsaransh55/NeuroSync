# NeuroSync — 7-Minute Jury Pitch (slide-by-slide + script)

> Format: **~7 min present + ~3 min Q&A**. Golden rule: **problem first, solution second.**
> Tell the human story, then *show* it working. Rehearse to land at **6:45**.
> Pair with `DEMO-SCRIPT.md` (live demo) and `QA-PREP.md` (Q&A).
> **You present and run Q&A live — AI must not present for you (jury rule).**

---

## SLIDE 1 — Title (0:10)
`NeuroSync` · *Work, in sync with how you think.* · team names.
**Say:** "Before we show anything we built, let us tell you about one ordinary Monday."

## SLIDE 2 — Meet Aanya (0:45)
*38 emails. 4 chat threads. 6 tickets. One hidden deadline.*
**Say:** Aanya — a talented analyst with ADHD and dyslexia. One email hides three tasks and a Thursday deadline in the third paragraph. She re-reads it four times, gets pulled away, and an hour later still hasn't *started*. **Nothing here is about her ability — the workday was built for a brain that isn't hers.**

## SLIDE 3 — The problem (1:00)
*"Turning workplace text into started, finished work carries a hidden 'cognitive tax' — far higher for ADHD and dyslexia."*
**Say:** Tasks and deadlines are buried in prose. Mainstream tools assume you read fast, hold it all in your head, and just start. The result: missed deadlines, burnout, and constant masking. This is wasted talent — not a niche wellbeing issue. *(source: 51% have taken time off — see Sources slide)*

## SLIDE 4 — Who, and how big (0:45)
**15–20%** neurodivergent · **~1 in 2** with ADHD also have dyslexia (one connected problem) · **Accenture's own research: +28% revenue, 2× net income, +30% margins** for inclusion leaders.
**Say:** "Accenture already proved inclusion pays. NeuroSync operationalises it for the neurodivergent talent already inside our walls."

## SLIDE 5 — NeuroSync + the 3 USPs (1:00)
*An inclusive work companion that turns chaos into clarity — adapted to how your brain works.*
1. **Adaptive output** — same message, rendered for *your* brain (a real per-user profile drives the AI).
2. **Understanding → doing** — we help you *start*, not just understand.
3. **Inclusion-first & enterprise-native** — calm by default, on Azure OpenAI in your tenant + your Outlook.
**Say (bridge):** "Instead of more slides — let us show you, personalised live."

## SLIDE 6 — LIVE DEMO (2:30) → follow `DEMO-SCRIPT.md`
Beats: **(a)** show the personalization profile; **(b)** paste Aanya's messy email → AI summary, action items, the hidden Thursday deadline, and a dyslexia-friendly rewrite — *rendered to her profile*; **(c)** "Convert to tasks" → one task, micro-steps with time-boxes; **(d)** Focus Mode; **(e)** "Feeling overwhelmed?" reset; **(f)** optional: pull the email straight from **Outlook**.
**Say (close):** "Same email. Now understood, broken down, started — rendered the way *her* brain works, and she never had to mask or ask."
*Keep a 60–90s backup recording ready (live AI over Wi-Fi).*

## SLIDE 7 — Why us, not Copilot/ChatGPT (0:30)
Table: *Adapts to the person · Helps you start · In-the-moment regulation · Runs on enterprise data* — ✅ NeuroSync vs ❌/partial others.
**Say:** "Generic AI gives everyone the same answer. NeuroSync adapts to the *person* — that's the moat."

## SLIDE 8 — Responsible & inclusive by design (0:30)
*No diagnosis · Opt-in profile · Human-in-the-loop · Sourced nudges · Inclusive by default.*
**Say:** "We never diagnose or label. The profile is a *choice* the user controls. The AI suggests; the human decides."

## SLIDE 9 — Feasibility & security (0:20)
*Working today on Azure: AI task-breakdown, summarizer, Projects, personalization, and real Outlook. Runs on **Azure OpenAI in-tenant** — client data stays in our boundary.*

## SLIDE 10 — Roadmap + ask (0:20)
*Next: Teams & Jira connectors, a validated ERG pilot, more language/low-vision profiles.*
**Say:** "Aanya doesn't need to be fixed. Her workday does. Help us pilot NeuroSync with a neurodiversity ERG. Thank you — we'd love your questions."
